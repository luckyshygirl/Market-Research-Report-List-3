<p><strong>The Ureteral Vascular Access Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CVC</li><li>PICC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ureteral Vascular Access Device market includes two types: Central Venous Catheters (CVC) and Peripherally Inserted Central Catheters (PICC). CVCs are long, flexible tubes inserted into a large vein near the heart, commonly used for administering medication or fluids or collecting blood samples. On the other hand, PICCs are catheters inserted through a peripheral vein in the arm, extending up to the central veins, and are preferred for long-term treatments like chemotherapy. Both devices play a crucial role in providing efficient vascular access, improving patient care and medical procedures.</p></p>
