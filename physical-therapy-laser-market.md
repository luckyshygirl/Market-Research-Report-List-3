<p><p>The Physical Therapy Laser market is categorized into two types: Fixed Type and Mobile Type. </p><p>Fixed Type refers to laser devices that are permanently installed in clinical settings or therapy centers. These lasers are usually more powerful and have a larger treatment area. They offer stability, accuracy, and convenience for therapists and patients within a specific location.</p><p>Mobile Type lasers, on the other hand, are portable devices that can be moved easily from one place to another. These lasers are lightweight and compact, allowing therapists to provide treatments at different locations or even during home visits. They offer flexibility and convenience for therapists and patients who require mobility in their therapy sessions.</p></p>
