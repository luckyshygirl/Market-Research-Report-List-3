<p><p>Linear AC Power Sources are traditional power sources that provide a smooth and continuous output waveform. They are known for their high accuracy and low distortion. PWM AC Power Sources, on the other hand, utilize pulse width modulation to control the output waveform, allowing for more efficient power conversion and precise control over the output voltage and frequency. Both types of AC power sources find applications in industries such as electronics manufacturing, automotive testing, and research laboratories.</p></p>
