<p><p>Passive Battery Cell Balancing is a method used to equalize the voltage levels of individual cells in a battery pack without the need for an active balancing circuit. This technique involves using passive components such as resistors or capacitors to dissipate excess energy from cells that are overcharged and transfer it to undercharged cells, thereby balancing the entire battery pack.</p><p>The Passive Battery Cell Balancing Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for battery management systems in various applications such as electric vehicles, consumer electronics, and renewable energy storage systems.
