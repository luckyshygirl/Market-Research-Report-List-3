<p><p>Industrial wrap films are commonly classified into three main types based on the material used for production: HDPE wrap films, LDPE wrap films, and PP wrap films. </p><p>HDPE wrap films are known for their high strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty industrial applications. LDPE wrap films, on the other hand, offer more flexibility and stretchability, making them suitable for wrapping irregularly shaped objects. PP wrap films are valued for their clarity and resistance to punctures, making them popular for displaying and protecting goods in retail settings.</p></p>
