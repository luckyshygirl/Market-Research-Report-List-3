<p><p>Colocation data center market can be categorized into two main types: retail colocation and wholesale colocation. Retail colocation refers to smaller data center spaces where businesses can rent a specific portion of the data center. This type is suitable for organizations with limited IT infrastructure needs. On the other hand, wholesale colocation involves leasing the entire data center space to a single tenant, typically a large enterprise or cloud service provider. Wholesale colocation is suitable for organizations with extensive IT infrastructure requirements or those planning to scale rapidly.</p></p>
