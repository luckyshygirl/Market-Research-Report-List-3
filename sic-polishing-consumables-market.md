<p><p>Silicon carbide (SiC) polishing consumables include SiC polishing slurries and SiC polishing pads. SiC polishing slurries are abrasive fluids used for polishing hard and brittle materials like ceramics and semiconductors. They help in achieving a smooth and reflective surface finish. SiC polishing pads are abrasive pads used in conjunction with the slurries for mechanical polishing processes. They provide a uniform and consistent polishing action, resulting in efficient material removal and improved surface quality. Both these consumables play a crucial role in achieving high-quality finishes in various industries.</p></p>
