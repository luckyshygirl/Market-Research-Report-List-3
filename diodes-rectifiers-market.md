Bridge rectifiers are used for converting alternating current to direct current in power supplies. PIN diodes are used as RF switches or attenuators. TVS diodes and ESD suppressors protect electronic circuits from voltage spikes. Current regulator diodes control the amount of current flowing through a circuit. Other types of diodes serve different functions in various electronic applications.</p></p>
