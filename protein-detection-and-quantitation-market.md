<p><p>Protein detection and quantitation market includes two main types - Instrument Detection and Reagent Detection. Instrument Detection involves the use of specialized instruments such as spectrophotometers and microplate readers to detect and quantify proteins. Reagent Detection, on the other hand, utilizes specific reagents or kits that react with proteins to produce measurable signals. Both types play a crucial role in various fields such as drug discovery, diagnostics, and research, providing researchers with accurate and reliable protein analysis.</p></p>
