<p><p>Self-adhesive papers are commonly used in both flexographic printing and letterpress printing markets. Flexographic printing involves a printing process where flexible relief plates are used to transfer ink onto the paper, making it ideal for high-volume production at high speeds. On the other hand, letterpress printing uses raised metal or polymer plates to create an impression on the paper, resulting in a distinctive and elegant finish. Both types of printing offer unique advantages and cater to different market needs within the self-adhesive paper industry.</p></p>
