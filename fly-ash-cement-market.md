<p><strong>The Fly Ash Cement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class F Fly Ash</li><li>Class C Fly Ash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fly Ash Cement is categorized into two types: Class F Fly Ash and Class C Fly Ash. Class F Fly Ash is produced from burning anthracite or bituminous coal and contains less than 5% calcium oxide. It is commonly used in concrete mixes for highways, bridge decks, and parking structures. Class C Fly Ash, on the other hand, is produced from burning lignite or sub-bituminous coal and contains more than 20% calcium oxide. It is suitable for applications where resistance to sulfates and chemicals is required, such as in wastewater treatment plants.</p></p>
