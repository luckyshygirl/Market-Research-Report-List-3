<p><p>The plastic pallet market is divided into two main types: HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) and PP (Polypropylene). HDPE pallets are known for their strength, durability, and stiffness, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. PP pallets, on the other hand, are lightweight, flexible, and resistant to cracking, making them suitable for lighter loads and one-way shipping. Both types of pallets are widely used in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and automotive for their cost-effectiveness and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
