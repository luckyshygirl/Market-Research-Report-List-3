<p><p>Mica Cable Tape market consists of two main types: Mica Glass Fiber Tape and Mica Polyester Tape. Mica Glass Fiber Tape is made by bonding mica paper with glass fiber cloth, providing excellent thermal and electrical insulation properties. On the other hand, Mica Polyester Tape is created by combining mica paper with polyester film, offering high flexibility and strength. Both types are widely used in the electrical industry for insulating cables and wires, ensuring safety and efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
