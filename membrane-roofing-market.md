<p><p>Membrane roofing market consists of three main types: synthetic rubber, thermoplastic membrane, and modified bitumen. Synthetic rubber membranes are durable, flexible, and resistant to UV rays. Thermoplastic membranes offer excellent heat-welded seams, making them popular for commercial buildings. Modified bitumen membranes are asphalt-based and have added polymers for enhanced durability and flexibility. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different roofing applications, providing customers with a range of options to choose from based on their specific needs.</p></p>
