<p><p>The Plant Substation Automation System (SAS) market includes two main types: Transmission Substations and Distribution Substations. </p><p>1) Transmission Substations play a crucial role in transferring high-voltage electricity from power plants to local distribution networks. SAS in these substations helps monitor and control power flow, ensuring grid stability and efficient transmission. </p><p>2) Distribution Substations, on the other hand, are responsible for stepping down the voltage to a suitable level for local consumers. SAS in these substations aids in monitoring and controlling power distribution, optimizing load management, and enhancing reliability of electricity supply to end-users.</p></p>
