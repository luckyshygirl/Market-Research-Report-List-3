<p><p>Pharmaceutical Hot Melt Extrusion Equipment market includes various types such as RAM Extruders, Radial Screen Equipment, and Roll and Screw Extruders. RAM Extruders use reciprocating action to push material through a die, while Radial Screen Equipment utilizes a rotating screen to mix and discharge materials. Roll and Screw Extruders combine both rolling and screwing actions to efficiently process materials. These different types of equipment cater to the diverse needs of pharmaceutical manufacturers for hot melt extrusion processes.</p></p>
