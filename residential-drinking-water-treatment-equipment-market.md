<p><p>The residential drinking water treatment equipment market includes various types of products to purify drinking water. Activated carbon filters use porous materials to remove impurities by adsorption. Reverse osmosis systems use a membrane to remove contaminants through a semipermeable barrier. Alkaline/water ionizers alter the pH level of water to make it more alkaline. UV filters use ultraviolet light to kill microorganisms in water. Other types of equipment include distillation systems, sediment filters, and water softeners, each addressing specific water quality concerns.</p></p>
