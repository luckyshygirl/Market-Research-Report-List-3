<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic PU</li><li>Thermoset PU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane synchronous belts can be classified into two main types based on their material: thermoplastic PU and thermoset PU. Thermoplastic PU belts are made from a material that can be softened and reshaped when heated, making them ideal for applications where flexibility is required. Thermoset PU belts, on the other hand, are made from a material that undergoes a chemical reaction during the manufacturing process, resulting in a more durable and heat-resistant belt. Each type has its own unique advantages and is suited for different applications in the market.</p></p>
