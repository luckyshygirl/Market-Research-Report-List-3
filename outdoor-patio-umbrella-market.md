<p><p>The outdoor patio umbrella market consists of three main types: wall mounted umbrellas, center pole umbrellas, and offset pole umbrellas. Wall mounted umbrellas are attached to a wall or structure, saving floor space and providing adjustable shade. Center pole umbrellas have a traditional design with a central pole supporting the canopy. Offset pole umbrellas have a pole that is located to the side, allowing for more flexible positioning and better coverage. Each type offers unique benefits and caters to different outdoor spaces and preferences.</p></p>
