<p><p>Sinking fish feed market is categorized into two main types: dry sinking fish feed and wet sinking fish feed. Dry sinking fish feed is formulated into pellets or granules and can be easily stored for longer periods of time. On the other hand, wet sinking fish feed is in the form of a paste or dough-like consistency and is usually more palatable for fish. Both types of sinking fish feed are designed to quickly sink to the bottom of the water for bottom-feeding fish species to consume.</p></p>
