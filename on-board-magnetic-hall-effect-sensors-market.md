<p><strong>The On Board Magnetic Hall Effect Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Below 1 Microgauss (Low-Field Sensors)</li><li>1 Microgauss to 10 Gauss (Earth Field Sensors)</li><li>Above 10 Gauss (Bias Magnetic Field Sensors)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>On-board magnetic Hall effect sensors are categorized into three market types based on their sensitivity to magnetic fields. Low-field sensors, with sensitivity below 1 microgauss, are ideal for detecting weak magnetic fields. Earth field sensors, with sensitivity ranging from 1 microgauss to 10 Gauss, are used for applications requiring measurement of the Earth's magnetic field. Bias magnetic field sensors, with sensitivity above 10 Gauss, are designed for detecting strong magnetic fields in various industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
