<p><p>All Dielectric Self-supporting Cable (ADSS) is a type of fiber optic cable designed for installation in distribution and transmission environments where traditional metallic grounded cables cannot be used. There are two main types of ADSS cables: Central Tube Structure ADSS and Layer Stranding Structure ADSS. Central Tube Structure ADSS has a central tube that houses the optical fibers, providing protection and support. Layer Stranding Structure ADSS consists of multiple layers of stranded fibers for added strength and durability. Each type has its own advantages depending on the specific installation requirements.</p></p>
