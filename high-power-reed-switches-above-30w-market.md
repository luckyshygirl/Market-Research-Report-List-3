<p><p>High Power Reed Switches (Above 30W) have two main types in the market - Normally Open Type and Normally Close Type. Normally Open Type switches are off by default and only close the circuit when a magnetic field is present. On the other hand, Normally Close Type switches are on by default and open the circuit when a magnetic field is detected. These switches are widely used in applications requiring high power handling capabilities, such as industrial machinery, automotive systems, and security systems.</p></p>
