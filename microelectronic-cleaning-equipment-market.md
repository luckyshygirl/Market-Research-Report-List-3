<p><ul><li>Single-Wafer Cryogenic Systems</li><li>Single-Wafer Spray Systems</li><li>Batch Immersion Cleaning Systems</li><li>Batch Spray Cleaning Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microelectronic cleaning equipment market includes various types such as Single-Wafer Cryogenic Systems, Single-Wafer Spray Systems, Batch Immersion Cleaning Systems, and Batch Spray Cleaning Systems. Single-Wafer Cryogenic Systems clean one wafer at a time using ultra-low temperatures. Single-Wafer Spray Systems use high-pressure nozzles to clean individual wafers. Batch Immersion Cleaning Systems clean multiple wafers at once by immersing them in a chemical solution. Batch Spray Cleaning Systems clean multiple wafers simultaneously by spraying them with cleaning solutions. Each type offers different benefits for cleaning microelectronic components.</p></p>
