<p><strong>The Maritime Sonar Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Beam Scanning Sonar System</li><li>Multi-beam Sonar System</li><li>Side Scan Sonar System</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Maritime Sonar Systems are categorized into different types based on their scanning capabilities. Single Beam Scanning Sonar Systems use a single beam to scan the water column for objects. Multi-beam Sonar Systems utilize multiple beams to provide a more detailed image of the underwater environment. Side Scan Sonar Systems are used to create detailed images of the seafloor and detect objects like wrecks or debris. Other types of sonar systems may include synthetic aperture sonar or forward-looking sonar, which offer unique scanning capabilities for specific applications in the maritime industry.</p></p>
