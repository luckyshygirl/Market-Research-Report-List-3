<p><ul><li>Heat Wire Method</li><li>Light Flash Method</li><li>Heat Flow Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thermal Conductivity Market consists of three main types: Heat Wire Method, Light Flash Method, and Heat Flow Method. The Heat Wire Method measures thermal conductivity by passing a current through a wire and measuring the temperature difference. The Light Flash Method heats a sample with a flash lamp and measures the temperature rise. Lastly, the Heat Flow Method measures thermal conductivity by applying heat to one side of a sample and measuring the temperature difference across it. These methods are employed to determine the thermal conductivity of various materials.</p></p>
