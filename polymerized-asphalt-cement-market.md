<p><strong>The Polymerized Asphalt Cement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer Polymer Modified Asphalt</li><li>Synthetic Resin Modified Asphalt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polymer Modified Asphalt is a type of polymerized asphalt cement where polymers, such as SBS, EVA, or EBA, are added to the asphalt binder to enhance its performance and durability. Synthetic Resin Modified Asphalt, on the other hand, involves the addition of synthetic resins like styrene-butadiene-styrene (SBS) or styrene-acrylate copolymer to the asphalt binder to improve its properties. Both types of modified asphalt are widely used in road construction and maintenance for their ability to increase resistance to rutting, cracking, and aging.</p></p>
