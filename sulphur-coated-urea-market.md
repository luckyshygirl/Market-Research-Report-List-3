<p><p>Sulphur Coated Urea comes in two main types: Polymer Sulphur Coated Urea and Wax Sulphur Coated Urea. Polymer Sulphur Coated Urea is created by binding sulphur with a polymer coating, providing a slow release of nitrogen over time. On the other hand, Wax Sulphur Coated Urea is made by encapsulating urea with a wax coating, allowing for a controlled and gradual release of nutrients. Both types offer advantages in terms of nutrient efficiency and environmental sustainability in agricultural applications.</p></p>
