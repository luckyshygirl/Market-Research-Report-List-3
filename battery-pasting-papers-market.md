<p><p>Battery pasting papers are used in the manufacturing of lead-acid batteries to enhance the performance and durability of the battery plates. There are two main types of battery pasting papers: cellulose pasting papers and fiberglass pasting papers. Cellulose pasting papers are made from natural fibers and are eco-friendly, while fiberglass pasting papers offer higher strength and resistance to acid corrosion. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the efficiency and longevity of lead-acid batteries in various applications.</p></p>
