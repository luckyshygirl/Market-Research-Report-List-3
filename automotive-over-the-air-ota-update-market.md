<p><strong>The Automotive Over-The-Air (OTA) Update Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Firmware Over-the-Air (FOTA)</li><li>Software Over-the-Air (SOTA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Over-The-Air (OTA) Update market consists of two main types: Firmware Over-The-Air (FOTA) and Software Over-The-Air (SOTA). FOTA refers to updates that focus on the vehicle's hardware and embedded systems, such as the engine control unit. On the other hand, SOTA involves updates to the software systems within the vehicle, including infotainment and driver assistance features. Both FOTA and SOTA enable automakers to remotely update and enhance the functionality, security, and performance of vehicles, improving the overall customer experience.</p></p>
