<p><p>The Document Content Management System (DCMS) market can be classified into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based DCMS refers to the software that is accessed and operated through the internet, allowing documents to be stored, managed, and shared remotely. On the other hand, an on-premise DCMS is installed and operated on a company's own servers, providing direct control over the storage and management of documents within the organization's premises. These two types cater to different organizational needs and preferences, offering varying benefits and challenges.</p></p>
