<p><p>The 100mm Silicon Wafer market has three main types: Prime Grade, Test Grade, and Monitor/Mechanical Grade. Prime Grade wafers are of the highest quality and are typically used for high-performance applications. Test Grade wafers are slightly lower in quality and are used for testing purposes. Monitor/Mechanical Grade wafers have the lowest quality and are primarily used for non-critical applications such as equipment calibration or mechanical testing. Each grade caters to different levels of performance and quality requirements in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
