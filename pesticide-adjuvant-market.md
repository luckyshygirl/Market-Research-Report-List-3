<p><p>Pesticide adjuvants are essential additives that enhance the performance and effectiveness of pesticides. The market can be categorized into various types including surfactants & emulsifiers, drift control agents, oil concentrates, compatibility agents, and water conditioners. Surfactants & emulsifiers help in the dispersion of pesticides, drift control agents prevent spray drift, oil concentrates improve pesticide adherence, compatibility agents ensure proper mixing of ingredients, and water conditioners optimize water quality for spray application. These adjuvants play a crucial role in maximizing the efficacy of pesticides for better pest control.</p></p>
