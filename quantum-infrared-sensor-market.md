<p><p>Quantum infrared sensors can be categorized into two main types: photon detection and thermal detection. Photon detection sensors operate by detecting individual photons of light, allowing for precise and sensitive measurement of light intensity. On the other hand, thermal detection sensors measure infrared radiation emitted by objects based on their temperature. Both types of sensors are commonly used in various applications such as night vision, surveillance, and medical imaging due to their high sensitivity and accuracy in detecting infrared radiation.</p></p>
