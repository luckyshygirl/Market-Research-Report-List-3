<p><strong>The Total Knee Arthroplasty Implants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed-bearing Implants</li><li>Mobile-bearing Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Total Knee Arthroplasty (TKA) implants are used in knee replacement surgeries to alleviate pain and improve function. The market for these implants can be classified into two types: fixed-bearing implants and mobile-bearing implants. Fixed-bearing implants have a fixed polyethylene insert between the femoral and tibial components. They provide stability, durability, and good long-term results. On the other hand, mobile-bearing implants have a polyethylene insert that can rotate between the femoral and tibial components. This type allows for better conformity and reduced wear, potentially improving longevity and range of motion.</p></p>
