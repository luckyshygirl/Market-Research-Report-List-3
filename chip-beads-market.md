<p><ul><li>High Impedance Chip Beads</li><li>Low Impedance Chip Bead</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip beads are electronic components used to suppress electromagnetic interference in electronic circuits. High impedance chip beads are designed to block high-frequency noise while allowing useful signals to pass through, making them ideal for applications requiring strict noise suppression. On the other hand, low impedance chip beads offer less resistance to signals, making them suitable for applications where signal integrity is crucial. Both types of chip beads help improve the performance and reliability of electronic devices by reducing noise and interference.</p></p>
