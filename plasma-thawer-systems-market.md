<p><ul><li>Dry Plasma Thawer</li><li>Wet Plasma Thawer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plasma thawer systems are used in medical facilities to thaw frozen plasma for transfusions. There are two main types: dry plasma thawers and wet plasma thawers. Dry plasma thawers use a combination of temperature-controlled air and heat radiation to thaw plasma without the need for water. Wet plasma thawers, on the other hand, utilize water baths to thaw frozen plasma. Both systems are designed to ensure proper and safe thawing of plasma, maintaining its quality and efficacy for patient use.</p></p>
