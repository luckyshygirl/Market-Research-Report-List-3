<p><p>Glass Fibre Reinforced Plastic (GFRP) market is divided into two main types: Continuous Fibers and Discontinuous (Short) Fibers. Continuous fibers provide greater strength and stiffness due to their continuous length, making them ideal for structural applications. Discontinuous fibers, on the other hand, are shorter in length and provide improved impact resistance and flexibility, making them suitable for applications where strength and flexibility are both important. Both types have their own unique benefits and are used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
