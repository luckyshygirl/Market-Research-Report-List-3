<p><ul><li>Asphalt Additives</li><li>Asphalt Modifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Asphalt additives are substances added to asphalt mixtures to improve their performance, durability, and workability. They can include materials such as polymers, anti-stripping agents, and rejuvenators. Asphalt modifiers, on the other hand, are used to alter the properties of the asphalt binder itself, such as improving its elasticity, reducing aging effects, and increasing temperature resistance. Both types of additives and modifiers play a crucial role in enhancing the quality and longevity of asphalt pavements in various applications.</p></p>
