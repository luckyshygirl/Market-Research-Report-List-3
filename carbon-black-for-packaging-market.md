<p><p>Carbon black for packaging can be divided into two main types based on their production method: Thermal carbon black products and Furnace carbon black products. Thermal carbon black products are produced through the controlled combustion of natural gas, resulting in high purity and low ash content. Furnace carbon black products are produced by burning heavy petroleum oil in a furnace, leading to a lower purity level but higher reinforcement properties. Both types of carbon black play a crucial role in enhancing the strength and durability of packaging materials.</p></p>
