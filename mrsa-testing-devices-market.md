<p><p>The MRSA testing devices market can be categorized into three types: immunodiagnostics, molecular diagnostics, and POC (point-of-care). Immunodiagnostics rely on the detection of specific antibodies to diagnose MRSA infections. Molecular diagnostics involve the use of DNA and RNA analysis to identify MRSA strains. POC testing refers to portable devices that can be used at the patient's bedside to provide rapid results. These three types of MRSA testing devices offer different approaches and benefits in terms of accuracy, speed, and convenience for healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
