<p><p>Biomedical pressure sensors are classified into various types such as Capacitive, Fiber Optic, Telemetric, Piezoresistive, and Others. Capacitive sensors measure pressure by detecting changes in capacitance, fiber optic sensors use light to measure pressure, telemetric sensors transmit pressure data wirelessly, and piezoresistive sensors utilize a semiconductor to measure pressure. Other types of pressure sensors may include optical, magnetic, or piezoelectric sensors. Each type of sensor offers unique advantages and applications in the biomedical field.</p></p>
