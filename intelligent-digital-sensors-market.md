<p><p>Insertion Intelligent Digital Sensors are designed to be inserted into a process stream for continuous monitoring and measurement of various parameters such as temperature, pressure, flow, and level. They are commonly used in industrial settings where real-time data is critical for process control and optimization. Immersion Intelligent Digital Sensors, on the other hand, are submerged directly into the substance being monitored, allowing for precise and accurate measurements in liquids or gases. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in enabling smart and efficient systems in various industries.</p></p>
