<p><p>The smoke extractor exhaust fan market is categorized into three main types. Centrifugal type fans have a blower wheel that helps in creating high-pressure airflow, making them suitable for applications with long duct systems. Axial type fans are designed with blades that force air to move parallel to the shaft, making them ideal for removing smoke or heat from enclosed spaces. Other types of smoke extractor exhaust fans may include mixed flow fans or forward curve fans, which have specific design features for diverse ventilation requirements.</p></p>
