<p><p>Coir market can be divided into two main types: Brown Fiber and White Fiber. Brown Fiber is extracted from mature coconut husks and is known for its durability and strength, making it suitable for products like ropes and mats. White Fiber, on the other hand, is obtained from younger coconut husks and is softer and more flexible, commonly used in products like mattresses and upholstery. Both types cater to different industries and applications within the coir market, offering a range of options for consumers.</p></p>
