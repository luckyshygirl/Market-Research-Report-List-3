<p><p>Silicone elastic gaskets come in various types, including Saddle and Waveform markets. Saddle gaskets are designed with a curved shape resembling a saddle, providing a secure seal in applications where pressure is a concern. On the other hand, Waveform gaskets have a wavy pattern that allows for flexibility and compression, making them ideal for irregular surfaces or joints. Both types of gaskets offer durability, resistance to extreme temperatures, and excellent sealing properties, making them popular choices in various industries.</p></p>
