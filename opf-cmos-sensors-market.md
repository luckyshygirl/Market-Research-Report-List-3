<p><ul><li>Linear Image Sensors</li><li>Area Image Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>OPF CMOS sensors refer to organic photoconductive film complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor sensors. These sensors are categorized into two main types: linear image sensors and area image sensors. Linear image sensors capture images in a single line format, making them ideal for applications such as barcode scanners and fax machines. Area image sensors, on the other hand, capture images in a grid format, making them suitable for devices like digital cameras and smartphones. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in modern imaging technology.</p></p>
