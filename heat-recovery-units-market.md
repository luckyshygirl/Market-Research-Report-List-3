<p><p>Heat recovery units are devices used to recover and exchange heat energy from exhaust air and transfer it to incoming fresh air. There are three main types: wall-mount, ceiling-mount, and cabinet-mount. Wall-mount units are installed on the walls and are generally compact and easy to install. Ceiling-mount units are installed on the ceiling and are suitable for spaces with limited wall space. Cabinet-mount units are enclosed in cabinets and are designed for larger commercial buildings or industrial settings. Each type has its unique benefits and can be chosen based on the specific requirements of the space.</p></p>
