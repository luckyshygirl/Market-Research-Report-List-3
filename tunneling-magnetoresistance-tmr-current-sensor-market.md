<p><p>The Tunneling Magnetoresistance (TMR) current sensor market can be categorized into two types: digital output and analog output. In digital output sensors, the measurement data is presented in a binary format, typically as a series of ones and zeros indicating the presence or absence of a current. Analog output sensors, on the other hand, provide continuous and proportional voltage or current signals corresponding to the measured current. Both types of sensors offer unique advantages and are utilized in various applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
