<p><strong>The Liquid Waterborne Printing Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexography Inks</li><li>Gravure Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid waterborne printing inks are classified into two main types: Flexography inks and Gravure inks. Flexography inks are specifically designed for use in flexographic printing, a popular method for packaging and label printing due to its versatility and cost-effectiveness. These inks are water-based and provide excellent print quality on a variety of substrates. On the other hand, Gravure inks are formulated for gravure printing, which is commonly used for high-volume printing jobs such as magazines and catalogs. Gravure inks offer fast drying times and vibrant colors for sharp and detailed prints.</p></p>
