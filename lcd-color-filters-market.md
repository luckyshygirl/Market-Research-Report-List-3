There are two main types of LCD Color Filters - CSTN-LCD Filters and TFT-LCD Filters. CSTN-LCD Filters are commonly used in low-cost displays, providing decent color reproduction and viewing angles. On the other hand, TFT-LCD Filters offer superior image quality, higher resolution, and better contrast ratios, making them ideal for high-end displays such as smartphones, tablets, and televisions. Both types of filters play a crucial role in enhancing the visual experience of LCD screens.</p></p>
