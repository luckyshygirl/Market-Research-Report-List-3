<p><ul><li>Self-Propelled Airports Passenger Stairs</li><li>Towable Airports Passenger Stairs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airports Passenger Stairs come in two main types: self-propelled and towable. Self-propelled stairs are equipped with their own engines and can move autonomously without the need for a towing vehicle. They are typically more convenient for operators as they can be moved around the airport independently. Towable stairs, on the other hand, require a towing vehicle to move them from one location to another. These stairs are usually more cost-effective and suitable for smaller airports or airlines with lower passenger traffic.</p></p>
