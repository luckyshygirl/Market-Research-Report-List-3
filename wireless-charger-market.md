<p><p>Wireless charger market is segmented into three types - Magnetic Resonance, Electromagnetic Induction, and Radio Frequency. Magnetic Resonance technology allows charging devices without direct contact through a magnetic field. Electromagnetic Induction technology transfers energy through electromagnetic fields between coils in the charger and device. Radio Frequency technology uses radio waves to transfer power wirelessly. Each type offers different benefits such as longer range (Radio Frequency), efficient charging (Electromagnetic Induction), and flexibility in placement (Magnetic Resonance) to cater to various charging needs.</p></p>
