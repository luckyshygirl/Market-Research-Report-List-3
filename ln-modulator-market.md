<p><p>High Frequency Modulators are designed for transmitting signals at higher frequencies, typically above 1 MHz. These modulators are ideal for applications such as satellite communication, radar systems, and wireless communication. On the other hand, Low Frequency Modulators operate at frequencies below 1 MHz and are commonly used in applications like audio transmission, telecommunication, and power line communication. Both types of modulators play a crucial role in signal processing and communication systems, catering to different frequency requirements and applications.</p></p>
