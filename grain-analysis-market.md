<p><p>Grain analysis involves testing for pathogens, pesticides, GMOs, and mycotoxins to ensure food safety and quality. Pathogens analysis focuses on detecting harmful bacteria or viruses that can contaminate grains. Pesticides analysis checks for presence of harmful chemicals used in agriculture. GMO analysis identifies genetically modified organisms in grains. Mycotoxins analysis is done to detect toxic substances produced by fungi that can impact human health. These different types of analysis help in maintaining the standards of grain products and ensuring consumer safety.</p></p>
