<p><strong>The Air Quality Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Signal-Gas Monitoring Sensor</li><li>Multi-Gas Monitoring Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air quality sensors market is categorized into two main types: signal-gas monitoring sensor and multi-gas monitoring sensor. Signal-gas monitoring sensors focus on detecting specific gases like carbon dioxide or volatile organic compounds. On the other hand, multi-gas monitoring sensors are designed to simultaneously monitor multiple gases in the air such as nitrogen dioxide, ozone, and particulate matter. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in monitoring and maintaining air quality in various indoor and outdoor environments.</p></p>
