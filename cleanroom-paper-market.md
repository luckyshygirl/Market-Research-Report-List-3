<p><ul><li>Cellulose Cleanroom Paper</li><li>Plastic Cleanroom Paper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cleanroom paper comes in two main types: cellulose and plastic. Cellulose cleanroom paper is made from wood pulp and is ideal for environments where low particle generation is crucial. It is highly absorbent and resistant to chemicals. Plastic cleanroom paper, on the other hand, is made from synthetic materials and offers excellent durability and tear-resistance. It is commonly used in environments with higher moisture levels or for tasks that require strong support. Both types of cleanroom paper are essential for maintaining cleanliness and preventing contamination in controlled environments.</p></p>
