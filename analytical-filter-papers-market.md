<p><p>Analytical filter papers are classified into three types based on their intended use: Qualitative Analytical Filter Papers, Quantitative Analytical Filter Papers, and Others. Qualitative filter papers are used for general filtration and particle retention, while Quantitative filter papers are designed for precise measuring and gravimetric analysis. The Others category includes specialized filter papers such as ashless filter papers, glass microfiber filter papers, and pore size specific filter papers. Each type caters to different analytical applications and requirements in scientific research and industries.</p></p>
