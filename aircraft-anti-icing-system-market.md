<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Aircraft Anti-icing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic De-icing Boots</li><li>Electro-thermal</li><li>Electro-mechanical</li><li>TKS Ice Protection</li><li>Bleed Air</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft Anti-icing Systems can be categorized into various types:</p><p>1. Pneumatic De-icing Boots: These systems use inflatable boots attached to the leading edges of the aircraft wings to break ice formation.</p><p>2. Electro-thermal Systems: These systems use electrical heating elements embedded in the aircraft's structure to prevent ice accumulation.</p><p>3. Electro-mechanical Systems: These systems use mechanical actuators to remove ice buildup on the aircraft's surfaces.</p><p>4. TKS Ice Protection: This system disperses a glycol-based fluid to prevent ice formation on the aircraft.</p><p>5. Bleed Air Systems: These systems use hot air from the engine's compressors to prevent ice buildup.</p><p>6. Passive Systems: These systems do not actively remove ice but rely on aerodynamic design to minimize ice accretion.</p></p>
