<p><p>The Evolved Packet Core (EPC) market consists of various components such as Policy and Charging Rules Function (PCRF), Packet Data Node Gateway (PGW), and Mobility Management Entity (MME). PCRF serves as the central policy control point for network data traffic, while PGW acts as the interface between the LTE network and external IP networks. MME manages the mobility of UE within the LTE network. These components play crucial roles in ensuring efficient data traffic management and seamless connectivity within LTE networks.</p></p>
