<p><strong>The Photochromic Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Organic</li><li>Inorganic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photochromic materials are divided into two main types: organic and inorganic. Organic photochromic materials are typically made from organic molecules containing conjugated double bonds, which undergo reversible structural changes when exposed to light. Inorganic photochromic materials, on the other hand, are made from inorganic compounds such as metal oxides or sulphides, which also undergo reversible changes in their electronic structure when exposed to light. Both types of materials have unique properties that make them suitable for various applications in eyewear, textiles, and other industries.</p></p>
