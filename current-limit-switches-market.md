<p><p>Current Limit Switches are devices that automatically shut off the power supply when the current exceeds a certain set limit. There are two main types in the market- Fixed Current Limit Switches and Adjustable Current Limit Switches. Fixed switches have a pre-set current limit that cannot be changed, while adjustable switches allow users to set their own desired current limit. Fixed switches are more commonly used in simple applications, while adjustable switches provide flexibility for more complex systems that require varying current limits.</p></p>
