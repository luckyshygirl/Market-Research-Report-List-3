<p><p>Solar ultra-clear float glass is categorized based on thickness into three main market types: below 3mm, 3mm to 5mm, and more than 5mm. Below 3mm glass is commonly used for applications such as thin film solar panels and electronic devices due to its lightweight and flexibility. Glass within the range of 3mm to 5mm is often used for architectural purposes like windows and doors. On the other hand, glass that is more than 5mm thick is typically used for structural applications where strength and durability are paramount, such as in solar power plants.</p></p>
