<p><strong>The Contact Smart Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active card</li><li>Passive card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact smart cards market can be classified into two types based on their functionality - active cards and passive cards. Active cards have an embedded power source which enables them to carry out complex functions and communicate independently with a reader. In contrast, passive cards require an external power source from the card reader to function, making them more cost-effective and simple in design. Both types of cards are widely used in various industries such as banking, healthcare, and transportation for secure access and transactions.</p></p>
