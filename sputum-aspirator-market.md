<p><p>The market for sputum aspirators includes two main types: electric sputum aspirators and manual sputum aspirators. Electric sputum aspirators are powered by electricity and are designed to automatically suction sputum from the airways. They are typically more efficient and provide consistent suction power. Manual sputum aspirators, on the other hand, are operated by hand using a control valve. They require manual suction and are commonly used in situations where electricity is not readily available. These two types cater to different needs and preferences within the market.</p></p>
