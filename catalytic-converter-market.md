<p><p>A catalytic converter is an emissions control device that converts toxic gases and pollutants in the exhaust of an internal combustion engine into less harmful emissions, such as carbon dioxide and water vapor. The device contains a catalyst, typically made of precious metals like platinum, palladium, and rhodium, which facilitate chemical reactions that break down harmful emissions.</p><p>The catalytic converter market is experiencing significant growth, driven by stringent emissions regulations around the world and the increasing adoption of cleaner technologies in automobiles.
