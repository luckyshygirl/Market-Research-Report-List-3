<p><strong>The Cranial Fixation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable Material</li><li>Non-Absorbable Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cranial Fixation Systems market offers two main types: Absorbable Material and Non-Absorbable Material. Absorbable Material fixation systems are made of materials that are gradually broken down and absorbed by the body over time. These systems are suitable for temporary use and do not require removal after healing. Non-Absorbable Material fixation systems are made of materials that remain intact and are not absorbed by the body. These systems are typically used for permanent fixation and may require surgical removal if needed.</p></p>
