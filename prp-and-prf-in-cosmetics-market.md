<p><p>PRP (platelet-rich plasma) and PRF (platelet-rich fibrin) are popular treatments in the cosmetics market. Pure-PRP is a concentration of platelets in plasma without red or white blood cells. Leukocyte-rich PRP contains a higher amount of white blood cells. Pure-PRF is a more advanced form of PRP, containing a higher concentration of platelets and leukocytes in a fibrin matrix. These products are used in various cosmetic procedures to stimulate collagen production and promote skin rejuvenation. Each type offers unique benefits for different skin concerns.</p></p>
