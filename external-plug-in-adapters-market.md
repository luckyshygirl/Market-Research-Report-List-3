<p><p>External plug-in adapters are commonly categorized into three main types: AC/AC, AC/DC, and DC/DC. AC/AC adapters convert alternating current from a wall outlet to a different voltage of alternating current. AC/DC adapters convert AC voltage to DC voltage, while DC/DC adapters convert one level of DC voltage to a different level of DC voltage. Each type of adapter serves a unique purpose and is used in various electronic devices to provide the necessary power conversion for proper operation.</p></p>
