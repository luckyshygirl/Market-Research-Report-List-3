<p><p>Froth floating chemicals market consists of three main types: Collectors, Frothers, and Modifiers. Collectors are chemicals that selectively attach to the mineral surfaces, making them hydrophobic and allowing them to float. Frothers create a stable froth layer on the surface of the flotation cell, aiding in the separation of minerals from the slurry. Modifiers are used to control the pH level of the pulp, adjust the chemical environment, and improve the overall efficiency of the flotation process.</p></p>
