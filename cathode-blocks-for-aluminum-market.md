<p><p>Cathode blocks for aluminum production come in three main types - Semi-Graphitic, Graphitic, and Graphitized. Semi-Graphitic blocks are made with a mix of graphite and carbonaceous materials, providing moderate thermal and electrical conductivity. Graphitic blocks have a higher graphite content, offering better electrical conductivity and resistance to oxidation. Graphitized blocks undergo further processing to increase graphite content, resulting in superior performance in terms of thermal conductivity, electrical resistivity, and overall durability. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences in the aluminum industry.</p></p>
