<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Concrete Containing Polymers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer Concrete (PC)</li><li>Latex-modified Concrete (LMC)</li><li>Polymer-impregnated Concrete (PIC)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete containing polymers is classified into various types based on the polymer used in the mixture. Polymer Concrete (PC) is made by blending polymers like epoxy or vinyl with cement. Latex-modified Concrete (LMC) incorporates latex emulsions to enhance flexibility and bonding strength. Polymer-impregnated Concrete (PIC) involves soaking the concrete with polymers to improve durability and resistance to chemicals. Other types may include polymer coatings or overlays. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of strength, flexibility, and durability for a wide range of construction applications.</p></p>
