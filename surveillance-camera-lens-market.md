<p><p>Surveillance camera lens market can be categorized into three main types: fixed lenses, varifocal lenses, and zoom lenses. Fixed lenses have a set focal length, offering a constant field of view. Varifocal lenses allow users to manually adjust the focal length for customized viewing angles. Zoom lenses provide the ability to adjust the focal length remotely, allowing for dynamic control over the field of view. Each type of lens caters to different surveillance needs, offering flexibility and versatility in monitoring environments.</p></p>
