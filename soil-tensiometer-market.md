<p><p>Soil tensiometers are devices used to measure soil moisture tension. There are two main types of soil tensiometers in the market: electronic reading and mechanical reading. Electronic reading tensiometers use sensors to measure soil tension and provide digital readings, making them more accurate and easier to use. On the other hand, mechanical reading tensiometers rely on a pressure gauge or dial to display soil tension readings. Both types have their advantages and are suitable for different applications depending on the user's preference and requirements.</p></p>
