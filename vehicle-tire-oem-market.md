<p><p>The Vehicle Tire OEM market is divided into two main types: Radial Tire and Bias Tire markets. Radial tires are constructed with layers of cord running perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing better stability and fuel efficiency. Bias tires, on the other hand, have cords that run diagonally across the tire, offering a more flexible ride at lower speeds. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences of consumers, with radial tires being more common in modern vehicles due to their performance benefits.</p></p>
