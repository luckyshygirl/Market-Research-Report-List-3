<p><p>The glass fiber reinforced PBT market can be divided into two main types: Injection Molding and Extrusion Molding. Injection molding involves melting the PBT resin and injecting it into a mold to create complex shapes, making it ideal for manufacturing intricate parts. On the other hand, extrusion molding involves forcing the molten PBT resin through a die to create continuous shapes like pipes or tubing. Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as automotive, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
