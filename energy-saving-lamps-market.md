<p><ul><li>Self-ballast Fluorescent Lamp (Electronic Energy Saving Lamp)</li><li>Single End Fluorescent Lamp (PL Plug in Energy Saving Tube Lamp)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Energy saving lamps come in various types, including self-ballast fluorescent lamps and single end fluorescent lamps. Self-ballast fluorescent lamps, also known as electronic energy saving lamps, have an integrated ballast that makes them easy to install and use. On the other hand, single end fluorescent lamps, also known as PL plug-in energy saving tube lamps, are designed for use in fixtures with separate ballasts. Both types of lamps are energy-efficient alternatives to traditional incandescent bulbs, helping consumers save on electricity costs.</p></p>
