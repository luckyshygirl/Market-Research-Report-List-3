<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Plastic Film Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Plastic Film Capacitors</li><li>Radial Plastic Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic Film Capacitors are categorized into two main types - Axial Plastic Film Capacitors and Radial Plastic Film Capacitors. Axial Plastic Film Capacitors are cylindrical in shape with leads extending from both ends, making them suitable for through-hole mounting on circuit boards. On the other hand, Radial Plastic Film Capacitors have leads that are attached to the same side of the capacitor, making them ideal for compact designs. Both types of capacitors are commonly used in various electronic applications due to their reliability, stability, and high capacitance values.</p></p>
