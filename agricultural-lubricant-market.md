<p><p>Agricultural lubricants are essential for maintaining machinery in the farming industry. Three main types of agricultural lubricants are mineral oil lubricants, synthetic lubricants, and bio-based lubricants. Mineral oil lubricants are derived from crude oil and are commonly used due to their affordability. Synthetic lubricants are man-made and offer superior performance and longevity. Bio-based lubricants are derived from renewable resources like plant oils and are environmentally friendly. Farmers can choose the best lubricant type based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
