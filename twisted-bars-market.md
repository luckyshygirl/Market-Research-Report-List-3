<p><p>Twisted Bars are commonly found in three main market types: Hot-rolling, Cold Rolling, and Cold Drawing. Hot-rolling involves shaping the bars at high temperatures, resulting in a rough finish. Cold Rolling, on the other hand, involves forming the bars at room temperature, giving them a smooth and polished appearance. Cold Drawing is a process where the bars are pulled through a die to achieve a precise size and shape. Each market type caters to different industries and applications depending on the required characteristics of the twisted bars.</p></p>
