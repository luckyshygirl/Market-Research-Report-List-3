<p><p>Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) Sample Preparation market types include Sequencing by Synthesis (SBS), Ion Semiconductor Sequencing, Single Molecule Real Time (SMRT) Sequencing, and Nanopore Sequencing. SBS involves detecting bases as they are added during DNA synthesis. Ion Semiconductor Sequencing measures the release of hydrogen ions as nucleotides are incorporated into a growing strand. SMRT Sequencing observes nucleotide incorporation in real-time using fluorescently-labeled nucleotides. Nanopore Sequencing passes DNA molecules through nanopores, measuring changes in electrical current to identify bases.</p></p>
