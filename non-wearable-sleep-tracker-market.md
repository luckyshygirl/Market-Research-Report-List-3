<p><p>Non-wearable sleep trackers can be categorized into three main types based on their placement: Under Mattress, Bedside, and Under Sheet trackers. Under Mattress trackers are placed directly under the mattress to monitor sleep patterns and movement. Bedside trackers sit on the nightstand and use sensors to track sleep metrics. Under Sheet trackers are embedded within the bedding to monitor sleep quality. Each type offers a non-intrusive way to track sleep without having to wear a device on the body.</p></p>
