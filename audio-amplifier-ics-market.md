<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audio Amplifier ICs are available in two main market types: Surface Mount and Through Hole. Surface Mount ICs are smaller in size and can be directly mounted onto the surface of a PCB, reducing the overall size of the circuit. Through Hole ICs are larger and have leads that need to be inserted into holes on the PCB for soldering. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on specific application requirements such as space constraints, ease of assembly, and cost considerations.</p></p>
