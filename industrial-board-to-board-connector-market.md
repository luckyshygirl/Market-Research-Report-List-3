<p><strong>The Industrial Board-to-Board Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-hole Technology</li><li>Surface-mount Technology</li><li>Plug-in Technology</li><li>Solderless Stacking Mezzanine Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial board-to-board connectors are available in various market types, including through-hole technology, surface-mount technology, plug-in technology, and solderless stacking mezzanine technology. Through-hole technology involves mounting components by inserting leads through holes in the PCB. Surface-mount technology attaches components directly to the surface of the PCB. Plug-in technology uses male and female connectors for easy assembly. Solderless stacking mezzanine technology allows for direct stacking of multiple connectors without the need for soldering, enabling quick and easy modular connections.</p></p>
