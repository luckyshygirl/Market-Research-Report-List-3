<p><p>Fusion Bonded Epoxy Coatings are utilized in two main markets: Internal Surface Coatings and External Surface Coatings. Internal Surface Coatings are applied to the inner surfaces of pipelines and tanks to protect against corrosion and improve flow efficiency. External Surface Coatings are applied to the outer surfaces of structures like pipelines and bridges to protect against environmental factors and prolong the lifespan of the infrastructure. Both types of coatings provide durable protection and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
