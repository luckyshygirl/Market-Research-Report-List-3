<p><ul><li>Rosin-based</li><li>Water soluble</li><li>No-clean</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronics soldering flux is available in three main types: rosin-based, water soluble, and no-clean. Rosin-based flux contains rosin as the main ingredient and is commonly used for general soldering applications. Water soluble flux can be easily cleaned with water after soldering, making it ideal for electronic components that must be thoroughly cleaned. No-clean flux leaves behind minimal residue after soldering, eliminating the need for post-soldering cleaning. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
