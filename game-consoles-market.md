<p><p>Game consoles can be categorized into two main types based on the control mechanism - handle control and somatosensory control. Handle control consoles are operated using handheld controllers with buttons, joysticks, and triggers, providing precise and traditional gameplay experience. On the other hand, somatosensory control consoles utilize motion sensors and cameras to allow players to control the game through body movements, gestures, and voice commands, offering a more immersive and interactive gaming experience. These two market types cater to different preferences and playstyles of gamers.</p></p>
