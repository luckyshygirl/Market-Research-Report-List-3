<p><p>Reciprocating PD pumps market comprises plunger pumps, piston pumps, and diaphragm pumps. Plunger pumps use a reciprocating plunger to move fluids, while piston pumps use a piston in a cylinder to create pressure. Diaphragm pumps use a diaphragm to displace the fluid. These pumps are commonly used in various industries for their ability to achieve high pressures and precise flow control. Each type of pump has specific applications based on their design and capabilities.</p></p>
