<p><ul><li>Straight Seam Welded Pipe</li><li>Spiral Welded Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Welded steel tube market consists of two main types: </p><p>1. Straight Seam Welded Pipe: This type of pipe is produced using a single continuous piece of steel, which is bent and welded along a straight seam. It is commonly used in various industries due to its cost-effectiveness and ease of production.</p><p>2. Spiral Welded Pipe: In this type, steel strips are coiled and welded together to form a spiral shape. It is known for its high strength and ability to withstand high pressures, making it suitable for applications such as oil and gas transportation and structural purposes.</p></p>
