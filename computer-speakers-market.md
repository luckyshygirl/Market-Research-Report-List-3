<p><p>Computer speakers come in two main market types - wired and wireless. On the other hand, wireless speakers use Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology to connect wirelessly to devices, offering more freedom of movement and placement. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences, with wired speakers being popular for their simplicity and reliability, while wireless speakers are favored for their convenience and versatility.</p></p>
