<p><p>Non-contact encoders are devices used to convert rotary motion into digital signals without the need for physical contact. The three main types of non-contact encoders are Magnetic Encoders, Capacitive Encoders, and Optical Encoders. </p><p>Magnetic encoders use magnets and sensors to detect changes in magnetic fields, Capacitive encoders use capacitive sensors to measure changes in capacitance, and Optical encoders use light to detect changes in position. Each type of encoder has its own advantages and applications in different industries such as robotics, automation, and automotive.</p></p>
