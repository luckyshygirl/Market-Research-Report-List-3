<p><strong>The Rain Light Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Type</li><li>Infrared Type</li><li>Resistive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rain light sensors are devices used in automobiles to automatically detect rain and control windshield wipers accordingly. There are three main types of rain light sensors in the market: capacitive type, which detects rain based on changes in capacitance; infrared type, which uses infrared light to detect raindrops; and resistive type, which measures changes in resistance caused by the presence of rain. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
