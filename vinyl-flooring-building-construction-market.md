<p><p>The vinyl flooring building construction market involves three main types of vinyl flooring: vinyl sheet, luxury vinyl tile (LVT), and vinyl composition tile (VCT). Vinyl sheet flooring is a continuous sheet of vinyl that is installed over a subfloor. LVT consists of individual tiles or planks that mimic the look of materials like wood or stone. VCT is composed of multiple layers of vinyl that are pressed together to create durable tiles. These types of vinyl flooring are commonly used in various construction projects, providing different aesthetic and functional options.</p></p>
