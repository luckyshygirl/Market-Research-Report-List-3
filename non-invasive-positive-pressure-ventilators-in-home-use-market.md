<p><strong>The Non-invasive Positive Pressure Ventilators (In-Home Use) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Level Ventilators</li><li>Bilevel Ventilators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-invasive positive pressure ventilators for in-home use are divided into two main market types: single level ventilators and bilevel ventilators. Single level ventilators provide a constant pressure throughout the breathing cycle, while bilevel ventilators offer two different levels of pressure - higher during inhalation and lower during exhalation. Single level ventilators are generally more cost-effective and simpler to use, while bilevel ventilators are often preferred for patients who require different levels of pressure to support their breathing.</p></p>
