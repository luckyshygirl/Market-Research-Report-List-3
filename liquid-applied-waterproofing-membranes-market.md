<p><p>Liquid applied waterproofing membranes are used to provide a protective barrier against water penetration for various surfaces. The market offers different types of membranes including bituminous, elastomeric, and cementitious. Bituminous membranes are made from asphalt and offer excellent waterproofing properties. Elastomeric membranes are flexible and can stretch to accommodate movement in the substrate. Cementitious membranes are made from cement and provide durable protection. Each type of membrane is designed to meet specific needs and requirements in the construction industry.</p></p>
