<p><p>Active Infrared Sensors come in two main types: Thermal and Quantum sensors. Thermal sensors detect infrared radiation emitted by an object due to its temperature, making them suitable for applications like motion detection and temperature monitoring. Quantum sensors, on the other hand, detect changes in infrared radiation caused by interruptions in the sensor's field, making them ideal for tasks like measuring distances and detecting objects. Both types of sensors are widely used in various industries such as security, automation, and automotive.</p></p>
