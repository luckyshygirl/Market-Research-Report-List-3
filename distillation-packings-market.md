<p><p>Distillation packings are used in chemical processing to increase the surface area for better separation of liquids. There are three main types of packings: metal, plastic, and ceramic. Metal packings are durable and have high thermal conductivity. Plastic packings are lightweight and resistant to corrosion. Ceramic packings are chemically inert and have high mechanical strength. Each type of packing has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the distillation process.</p></p>
