<p><p>Building-Integrated Photovoltaic (BIPV) technology includes three main market types: single crystal silicon, polycrystalline silicon, and thin film. Single crystal silicon panels are known for their high efficiency, durability, and long lifespan. Polycrystalline silicon panels offer a balance between cost and efficiency. Thin film panels are lightweight, flexible, and can be integrated seamlessly into building materials. Each market type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications, depending on factors such as budget, space availability, and aesthetic preferences.</p></p>
