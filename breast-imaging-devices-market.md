<p><strong>The Breast Imaging Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ionized Breast Imaging Device</li><li>Non Ionized Breast Imaging Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Breast imaging devices are used for the detection and diagnosis of breast abnormalities. Ionized breast imaging devices, such as mammography and tomosynthesis, utilize low-dose ionizing radiation to create detailed images of the breast tissue. They are widely used due to their accuracy in detecting breast cancer. Non-ionized breast imaging devices, including ultrasound and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), use sound waves or magnetic fields to generate images. These devices are often used for follow-up examinations or for individuals with dense breasts. Both types play a crucial role in early detection and effective management of breast diseases.</p></p>
