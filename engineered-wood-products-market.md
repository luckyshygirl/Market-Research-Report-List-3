<p><p>Engineered Wood Products (EWP) are wood products manufactured by binding together wood fibers, strands, or veneers with adhesives to create a stronger and more versatile material than traditional solid wood. These products are used in various construction applications due to their durability, sustainability, and cost-effectiveness. Common examples of EWP include plywood, oriented strand board (OSB), laminated veneer lumber (LVL), and glued laminated timber (glulam).</p><p>The Engineered Wood Products Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by factors such as increasing demand for sustainable construction materials, growing awareness about the benefits of EWPs, and technological advancements in the industry.
